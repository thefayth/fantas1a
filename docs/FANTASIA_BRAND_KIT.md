# Fantasia Brand Kit

Fantasia is a premium local-first AI operations desktop. The brand should feel like a private command room: composed, intelligent, magical without being childish, and serious enough for investor diligence.

## Brand Essence

**Positioning:** Local AI operations desktop for builders who need automation with receipts, rollback, and control.

**Promise:** AI with hands, memory, receipts, and brakes.

**Tone:** Warm operator, sharp strategist, private command room, founder-grade.

**Avoid:** Generic SaaS gradients, cute mascots, cartoon magic, fake AI futurism, loud crypto styling, vague productivity language.

## Visual System

### Type Direction

- Headlines: high-confidence grotesk or editorial sans, heavy weight, tight but not compressed.
- Body: readable modern sans.
- Data labels: small uppercase with generous spacing.
- Avoid negative letter spacing.

### Shape Language

- Cards and panels use 8px radius or less.
- Status chips can be pill-shaped.
- Use clean grid systems, not floating decorative cards.
- Receipts, ledgers, and proof states should look tactile and auditable.

## Logo Direction

Primary mark concept:

```text
F inside a seal-like command glyph
```

The mark should combine:

- private operator cockpit
- proof seal
- controlled magic
- local desktop authority

Do not use a wand, fairy, cartoon sparkle, or childish fantasy icon.
